Who I am

Grounded in data.
Driven by real institutions.

I'm a final-year BS Information Technology student majoring in Business Analytics at Batangas State University TNEU – Lipa Campus. My focus sits at the intersection of full-stack development, LLM integration, and data analytics — I like building software that doesn't just work, but measurably fixes something broken.

My flagship project, ATLAS, is a multi-tenant instructional management platform that automates OBE compliance for Philippine higher-ed faculty. It cut syllabus prep time by more than 60% and earned a special award for requirements engineering — proof that I can take a messy institutional process, break it down properly, and ship something faculty actually use.

Outside of code, I lead a 500+ member tech organization as Chief Communications Officer, play competitive badminton, and once took first place in a coffee painting competition — because precision under pressure shows up in more places than just a codebase.
